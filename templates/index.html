Activity Blog

At the start of June 2025, I challenged myself to walk 10,000 steps a day for the entire month. I posted the distance and number of steps to Twitter every day in an effort to keep myself honest.


I succeeded and found out a couple of interesting things about myself:


  • The exercise was very beneficial for my physical and mental health and for managing my ADHD
  • By posting to Twitter, I was making myself accountable which was incredibly effective in not just letting things slide

In addition, I was losing weight very quickly, which is a good thing as I was a bit of a blob.


Rather than relentlessly boring my friends on Twitter, I decided to start this blog, both as a way to carry on the above benefits, but also to force myself to learn new things. From a very rudimentary knowledge of basic HTML, I am now self-hosting this site on a Raspberry Pi in my living room and have built this site using Python, Flask, nginx and gunicorn!
